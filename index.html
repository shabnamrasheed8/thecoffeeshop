The Coffee Shop

The Coffee Shop is one of the oldest coffee houses in Sri Lanka. We currently have 7 branches and is growing rapidly. Coffee is a journey, and we welcome you to join us for the ride.

Our History


The Coffee Shop is a startup which was created to cater to the true authentic coffee house experience, by emphasizing on the high-quality coffee, which we have not compromised whatever the situation we faced. The true reason for the success of The Coffee Shop is its work force, some employees being a part of the team since its inception.


With our humble beginnings at Borella, throughout these five years The Coffee Shop has grown strength to strength expanding our branches to Barns Place, Nawala, Bambalapitiya, Kandy, Mount Lavinia and Nugegoda with ideas of expanding even further to ensure that a good quality cup of coffee is just within your reach.
